📄 Methodology

TrustLex is a research visualization tool powered by the Database of AI Litigation (DAIL), curated by GW Law's Ethical Technology Initiative (Victoria Neal). Jurisdiction is resolved from Jurisdiction_Name → Current_Jurisdiction → Jurisdiction_Filed, including county-level court strings. When secondary sources are provided, cases link directly to coverage articles. All data is presented for research and policy awareness — not legal advice.
